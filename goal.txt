You open up the executable.
A screen pops up.
Some text fades in and fades out.
The main menu fades in.


Main menu:
	Normal Mode
	Coin Mode
	Options

Normal mode and Coin mode:
	Choose the universe (icons),
	then choose the world (icons),
	then choose the level (numbers).

Options:
	Sound volume (bar)
	Music volume (bar)
	Difficulty level (1,2,3)


When you select a level to play:
	Show story if first level in world.
	Then start playing the level.

When you finish a level:
	go to next level if doesn't change world.
	else go to next world if doesn't change universe (show story first).
	else show ending for this universe and then go back to main menu.
